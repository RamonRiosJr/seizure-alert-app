The app is designed to adhere to **Level AA** standards.

- **Keyboard Nav:** The interface acts as a "Big Button" accessible interface.

## FDA Regulatory Status

**Classification:** General Wellness / Non-Device

- **Policy:** FDA General Wellness: Policy for Low Risk Devices
- **Status:** Enforcement Discretion (Exempt from 510(k) Premarket Notification)
- **Rationale:** This app functions as a "Reference" and "Manual Diary". It does not perform automated diagnosis or monitoring.
